How to Play

Welcome to the game! You're taking on the role of a detective. Try to find the clues and solve the mystery.

Here's how to play:

  1. Click "Start Game"
  2. Search the crime scene carefully
  3. Click object to uncover clues
  4. Solve the puzzle to collect words
  5. Use the collected words to solve the mystery
